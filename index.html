Ella

Ella, a scenic town in Sri Lanka, is famous for its lush tea plantations, breathtaking views, and iconic Nine Arches Bridge.

Sigiri

Sigiriya, an ancient rock fortress in Sri Lanka, is renowned for its impressive frescoes, water gardens, and panoramic views from its summit.

Arugam Bay

Arugam Bay in Sri Lanka is a renowned surfing hotspot, celebrated for its beautiful beaches and lively coastal atmosphere.
